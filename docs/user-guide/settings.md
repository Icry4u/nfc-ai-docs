# Settings & Preferences

The Settings screen lets you customize the app to fit your needs. You can
access it by tapping the **gear icon** on the main screen.

---

## User Profile

### Name

Your display name, entered during initial setup. You can change it at any time
from the Settings screen.

- Tap the name field to edit.
- Your name is used to personalize the experience.

---

## Dietary Restrictions

Manage your food restrictions and allergy information. These settings directly
affect how the app flags allergens and how AI recommendations are filtered.

| Option            | What It Controls                                                    |
|-------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Dairy-Free**    | Flags dairy allergens. AI avoids recommending dairy products.        |
| **Gluten-Free**   | Flags gluten allergens. AI avoids recommending gluten-containing items. |
| **Low-Sodium**    | Highlights high-sodium products. AI prefers lower-sodium alternatives. |
| **Vegan**         | Flags all animal-derived ingredients. AI only suggests plant-based items. |
| **Nut Allergy**   | Flags nut allergens. AI strictly bans all nut-containing products.   |

Toggle each option on or off. You can enable multiple options at the same time.

!!! warning
    Changes to your dietary restrictions take effect immediately. The next
    product you scan will use the updated profile for allergen detection
    and AI recommendations.

!!! tip
    If you are shopping for someone else (for example, a family member with
    allergies), update the dietary profile to match their needs before scanning
    products for them.

## Appearance

### Theme

Choose how the app looks:

| Theme       | Description                                                         |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **System**  | Follows your Android system setting. If your phone is in dark mode, the app will be too. |
| **Light**   | Always uses a light background with dark text.                       |
| **Dark**    | Always uses a dark background with light text. Easier on the eyes in low light. |

### Font Size

Adjust the text size throughout the app:

| Size        | Best For                                                           |
|-------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Small**   | Users who want to see more content on screen at once.               |
| **Medium**  | The default setting. Balanced readability for most users.           |
| **Large**   | Users who need bigger text for easier reading. Recommended for visually impaired users. |

!!! tip
    The **Large** font size pairs well with the **Dark** theme for maximum
    readability, especially for users with low vision.

## Speech Settings

### Speech Speed

Control how fast the text-to-speech voice reads product information:

| Speed    | Description                                                         |
|----------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **x0.5** | Half speed. Very slow and deliberate. Good for detailed listening.   |
| **x1.0** | Normal speed. The default.                                          |
| **x1.5** | Faster speed. For experienced users who want quicker readouts.      |

### Auto TTS

A toggle switch that controls whether the app automatically reads product
information aloud after every scan.

- **On** — Product details are spoken automatically as soon as a scan completes.
- **Off** — The app stays silent after a scan. You can still tap the Speak Aloud
  button to hear information on demand.

!!! note
    Turning off Auto TTS does not disable the Speak Aloud button. You always
    have the option to hear product information by tapping the floating action
    button on the product detail screen.

---

## AI Settings

### AI Toggle

Enable or disable the AI recommendation feature:

- **On** — The app fetches AI-powered alternative product suggestions after
  each scan. Requires internet.
- **Off** — No recommendations are shown. Only scanned product details appear.

!!! note
    Disabling AI can be useful if you are in an area with no internet and do
    not want to see the "AI Recommendations require internet" message
    after each scan.

## Reset Options

The Settings screen includes three reset options for managing your app data
and experience.

### Reset Tutorial

- Tap this button to re-enable the **spotlight tutorial** that appeared on your
  first launch.
- The next time you return to the main scanner screen, the tutorial will play
  again, highlighting each part of the interface.
- Useful if you forgot how a feature works or want to show someone else how
  the app operates.

### Reset to Defaults

- Restores **all settings** to their original default values.
- This includes: theme (System), font size (Medium), speech speed (x1.0),
  Auto TTS (On), AI (On), and dietary restrictions (all unchecked).
- Your name is kept.

!!! warning
    A **confirmation dialog** appears before the reset takes effect. You must
    confirm by tapping "Reset" in the dialog. Tap "Cancel" to keep your
    current settings. This action cannot be undone.

### Clear History

- Removes all previously scanned products from your scan history.
- The product database is not affected — you can scan the same products again.

!!! warning
    A **confirmation dialog** appears before clearing your history. Once
    confirmed, your scan history is permanently deleted and cannot be
    recovered.
